## Canva Workflow: LinkedIn Image + Text

1. Open Canva, create 1080x1080px (square) or 1200x628px (landscape)
2. Upload AI-generated image as background
3. Add dark gradient overlay (black, 40-60% opacity) on bottom third
4. Place hook text in white (bold sans-serif, 44-56pt) over the dark area
5. Optional: Add "Camino Coaching" small watermark bottom corner (white, 25% opacity)
6. Export as PNG

**For text-on-black (System 1):**
1. Black background (#000000)
2. White text, centred, maximum 3 lines
3. Bold sans-serif font (Montserrat Black, Arial Black, or DM Sans Bold)
4. LinkedIn-appropriate: slightly more refined than Facebook. Consider dark navy (#0A1628) instead of pure black for a more executive feel
5. Export as PNG
